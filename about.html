About

Greetings mga kaibigan (friends)! This is a food blog, to share the best Filipino and/or other cuisine recipes that tickles my tongue. I am originally from the Philippines but moved to Texas, USA at age of 9. However, my mom have constantly cooked for us growing up and the filipino flavor have been ingrained into my being. So, as a huge fan of the Filipino way of cooking, it is my goal to guide people how to cook Filipino dishes in the simplest and enjoyable way.

Filipino cuisine have been gaining some attention but I still want to continually introduce peeple to the tastes of our dishes. And I am hoping this food blog will be a gateway for more people to be more open and possibly capture a new favorite cusine. So come on, mga kaibigan! Chibogan na!
