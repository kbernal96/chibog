Chibog (chi-bog)

A Filipino slang word that simply means food or meal time. It is commonly heard at informal Filipino parties and gatherings. When the guests have arrived and the food is ready, expect to hear the host to say "chibogan na!" (eating time!).

Sisig

A traditional pork dish in the Philippines served in a hot sizzling plate. Often served as either a main course, snack or just a salad.

Kare Kare

A household favorite! A Filipino stew with a rich and thick peanut sauce. It is a popular dish in the Philippines served during special occasions.
